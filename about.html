About Us

We provide affordable and reliable technology solutions in South Africa.

Mido's tech gadgets and accessories is a small retail store that specialises in computer and hardware sales. Our focus is to create a website for our store to market its products and provide important business information to our online customers. Mido's computer shop was established in 2019 in Mthatha, growing from a small, semi-developed start up to a leading SA online retailer of computer hardware and tech accessories, driven by a zest for tech and great services.

Our Mission

Our Vision

Become a trusted tech provider across Africa.
